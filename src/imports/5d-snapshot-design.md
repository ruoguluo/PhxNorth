Design a 5D Snapshot panel for a SaaS mentorship and enterprise platform.

This is NOT a personality test.
This is a dynamic identity intelligence engine.

The snapshot must:

• Use a 5-dimension radar/star chart
• Focus on growth logic and behavior visibility
• Avoid personality labels
• Avoid referencing DISC
• Avoid showing risk profile

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — 5D STAR RADAR
--------------------------------------------------

Five dimensions:

• Capability Depth
• Execution Pattern
• Decision Orientation
• Collaboration Style
• Growth Trajectory

Display as radar chart.

Show clarity levels (High / Medium / Emerging).
Not score-based evaluation.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — GROWTH LOGIC INSIGHTS
--------------------------------------------------

Show structured insights:

Career Pattern Recognition:
• Responsibility expansion trend
• Role transition logic
• Exit pattern consistency

Platform Interaction Signals:
• Mentorship frequency
• Engagement depth
• Knowledge evolution

Behavioral Visibility:
• Profile completeness %
• Decision pattern confidence
• Interaction depth

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — DATA GAPS & VISIBILITY OPPORTUNITIES
--------------------------------------------------

Show what is missing:

"We currently have limited visibility on:"

• Decision-making under uncertainty
• Cross-functional collaboration
• Strategic consistency

Add CTA buttons:
• Participate in structured mentorship
• Join enterprise project
• Add detailed responsibilities
• Increase interaction depth

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — MATCHING READINESS
--------------------------------------------------

Show readiness indicators:

• Mentorship Matching
• Talent Referral
• Enterprise Project Matching

Use High / Medium / Developing scale.

--------------------------------------------------
Design style:

• Clean SaaS
• Analytical, structured
• Professional
• No playful psychology tone
• Emphasize business matching intelligence
• Show "Last updated based on X interactions"
