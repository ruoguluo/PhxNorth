Design a multi-step “Create Account” onboarding flow for an AI-powered human capital platform.

The onboarding must feel professional, structured, and enterprise-grade.

------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — Basic Information

Fields:

• Username
• Full Legal Name
  - Include checkbox: “Keep my real name private”
• Status (required selection):

  1. Currently Studying
     - Degree Level (dropdown)
       • Bachelor
       • Master
       • MBA
       • PhD
       • Executive Education
     - Major / Field of Study (dropdown)

  2. Working Professional
     - Years of Experience (dropdown)
       • 1–3 years
       • 3–5 years
       • 5–10 years
       • 10+ years

------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — Location & Market Focus

• Current Country / Region (dropdown)
• Interested Countries / Markets (multi-select dropdown)

Include helper text:
“This helps us match you with relevant mentors, projects, and enterprise opportunities. You can update this anytime.”

------------------------------------------------
STEP 3 — Industry Selection (Hierarchical Dropdown)

Design a 3-level structured industry taxonomy:

Level 1 — Major Industry
Level 2 — Sector
Level 3 — Sub-sector

Example structure:

1. Technology
   - Software
     • SaaS
     • AI / Machine Learning
     • Cybersecurity
   - Hardware
     • Semiconductors
     • IoT
   - FinTech
     • Payments
     • Digital Banking

2. Finance
   - Investment Banking
   - Asset Management
   - Private Equity
   - Wealth Management

3. Healthcare
   - Pharmaceuticals
   - Medical Devices
   - Biotech
   - Healthcare IT

4. Energy & Infrastructure
   - Renewable Energy
     • Solar
     • Wind
   - Oil & Gas
   - Utilities
   - Construction & Engineering

5. Consumer & Retail
   - FMCG
   - E-commerce
   - Luxury
   - Food & Beverage

6. Manufacturing
   - Automotive
   - Aerospace
   - Industrial Equipment
   - Chemicals

7. Professional Services
   - Consulting
   - Legal
   - Accounting
   - Executive Search

Allow:

• Current Industry (required)
• Interested Industries (multi-select)

------------------------------------------------
STEP 4 — Account Security

• Email
• Password
• Confirm Password

After submission:
Send verification email.

Display message:
“Please verify your email to activate your account.”

After email confirmation:
Redirect to Member Dashboard.
